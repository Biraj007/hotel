About Us

Founded in 2011, ADB Kanvas began with a vision to redefine hospitality in India. With over a decade of excellence, we take pride in operating premier resorts in Mandarmani and Lataguri. Guided by our motto "Atithi Devo Bhava" (The Guest is God), we treat every visitor with the utmost respect and care. Our commitment to quality service, guest satisfaction, and memorable experiences has established ADB Kanvas as a trusted name in luxury beach and jungle retreats.

Know Us Better

We currently operate in two prime locations Mandarmani (Beach) and Lataguri (Jungle) with a third exciting destination launching soon. ADB Kanvas is renowned for its guest-centric approach, offering premium amenities like swimming pools, spacious sea-facing rooms, and private beach access.

Despite challenges along our journey, our unwavering commitment to guest satisfaction has fueled our growth. Today, our loyal guests are our biggest ambassadors, sharing their memorable experiences and recommending ADB Kanvas far and wide. We are truly grateful for this incredible bond and continued trust.

Serenity by the Sea: The Coastal Escape

Nestled just 180 km from Kolkata, ADB Kanvas Mandarmani is where the horizon meets luxury. Wake up to the rhythm of the waves in our premium sea-facing rooms and walk straight onto the longest drive-in beach in India. Whether you are lounging by our infinity pool or enjoying a sunset boat cruise, we offer a sanctuary of calm amidst the salt air. Experience the ultimate beachside hospitality where the ocean is your front yard.

Adventure in the Wild: The Jungle Retreat

Immerse yourself in the heart of the Dooars at ADB Kanvas Lataguri. Surrounded by the whispering trees of the Gorumara Forest, our resort blends modern luxury with raw nature. It is the perfect base camp for thrill-seekers from elephant safaris to riverside relaxation by the Murti River. After a day of exploration, return to the comfort of our resort, where the sounds of the jungle lull you to sleep.
