RESORT AMENITIES

Hotel Services & Amenities in Mandarmani

At ADB Kanvas Resort in Mandarmani, we believe that exceptional hospitality goes beyond just comfortable rooms. Our resort offers a comprehensive range of premium amenities designed to make your beach vacation truly luxurious. Whether you want to unwind at our spa with relaxing massages, stay fit at our modern gym, or take a refreshing dip in our swimming pool, every facility is crafted for your comfort. Our multi-cuisine restaurant serves delectable local Bengali delicacies alongside international favorites, while our banquet hall is perfect for destination weddings and corporate events. Located on the pristine Mandarmani beach, just a short drive from Digha and Shankarpur, ADB Kanvas is the ultimate choice for families, couples, and groups seeking a memorable seaside escape.
