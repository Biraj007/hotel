How to Reach Mandarmani from Kolkata

By Car: Take the Kona Expressway from Kolkata for 172 km until you reach Kolaghat. Turn left towards Digha. About 2 km before Contai Town, take the Contai Bypass road. Continue for another 10 km to Chawalkhola, then take a left turn onto the village road leading straight to Mandarmani.

By Bus: Catch a bus from Esplanade or Howrah heading to Chawalkhola or Digha. From Chawalkhola, auto-rickshaws and hired cars are readily available to take you to the resort. Volvo buses from Gariahat via Esplanade are also popular options for a comfortable journey.
