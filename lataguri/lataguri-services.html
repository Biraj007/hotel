Hotel Services & Amenities in Lataguri

At ADB Kanvas Resort in Lataguri, we offer a harmonious blend of jungle charm and modern comfort. Nestled at the edge of Gorumara National Park, our resort provides world-class amenities amidst the natural beauty of the Dooars. Refresh yourself in our sparkling swimming pool, savor authentic Bengali and multi-cuisine dishes at our restaurant, or host memorable events in our elegant banquet hall. Whether you're here for a family vacation, a romantic getaway, or a corporate retreat, our dedicated staff ensures every moment is special. Located near Jaldapara and Chapramari Wildlife Sanctuary, ADB Kanvas is the preferred choice for travelers seeking comfort in the heart of North Bengal's jungle paradise.
