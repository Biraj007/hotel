Jungle Activities & Adventures in Lataguri

Lataguri is the gateway to the magnificent Gorumara National Park and the enchanting Dooars region, offering nature lovers and wildlife enthusiasts an unforgettable experience. At ADB Kanvas Resort, we curate the perfect jungle getaway with activities that bring you closer to nature. Embark on thrilling jungle safaris to spot one-horned rhinos and elephants, enjoy bird watching sessions in the tea gardens, or simply unwind on our serene forest deck. Our resort is ideally located near Jaldapara, Chapramari, and Buxa Tiger Reserve, making it the perfect base for exploring North Bengal's wildlife. From evening bonfires under the starlit sky to nature walks through the forest trails, every moment at Lataguri becomes a treasured memory.
