How to Reach Lataguri from Kolkata

By Air: Fly from Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose International Airport in Kolkata to Bagdogra Airport, located just 80 km from Lataguri. From there, you can hire a taxi or use our convenient shuttle service to reach the resort.

By Train: Take a train from Howrah or Sealdah to New Jalpaiguri (NJP), located 60 km from Lataguri. From NJP, a taxi or our pick-up service will bring you directly to our Dooars hotel.

By Road: For those who love road trips, enjoy a scenic 600 km drive via NH12. The route offers beautiful views of the Dooars region along the way, setting the mood for your jungle adventure.
